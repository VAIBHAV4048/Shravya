Shravya provides real-time subtitle for your calls and allows you to communicate through both text and voice. It's quick, secure, and precise.

About

Shravya leverages artificial intelligence (AI) to seamlessly transform text-to-speech and speech-to-text in real time. Our technology enables effortless calling and receiving without the need for hearing or speaking. Instead, you can communicate by typing and reading.

Own Your Calls.

Forget about human-operated relay services. Shravya does phone call captioning in real-time while keeping calls 100% private. No Interpreters, stenographers, or assistants involved.

Your call transcripts are stored locally on your device, keeping them 100% private. You’ll never have to worry about forgetting important details again.
